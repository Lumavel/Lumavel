## What is Lumavel?

**Lumavel** builds tools and marketplaces for **handmade sellers**, **independent creators**, and **small online businesses**.

Our flagship product is **[Lumavel Shopping Plaza](https://lumavel.co/lumavelshoppingplaza)** — a curated marketplace on [lumavel.co](https://lumavel.co) where sellers get storefronts, marketing tools, and transparent pricing without the algorithm chaos of legacy platforms.

Around the Plaza, we ship an integrated **creator ecosystem**: QR marketing, hosted portfolios, printable generators, and **Prism** (Lumavel’s digital archive and creator foundation). One brand, one mission: **help creators succeed online and keep more of what they earn.**

---

## Creator ecosystem mission

We believe independent creators deserve:

| Principle | What it means at Lumavel |
|-----------|---------------------------|
| **Fair economics** | **1%** platform maintenance on Plaza sales (vs. ~6.5% on Etsy-style platforms), predictable shelf pricing, Stripe processing passed through |
| **Owned relationships** | Sellers build **their** email lists and customer connections — not rented audience on a black-box feed |
| **Honest tools** | Marketing, SEO, QR, and analytics that support the shop — not upsells bolted on after signup |
| **Long-term craft** | Curated onboarding, community feedback, and software built for people who make things by hand |

**Current focus:** grow a healthy seller community on Shopping Plaza and refine the tools that make selling and marketing sustainable.

## Flagship: Shopping Plaza

**[lumavel.co/lumavelshoppingplaza](https://lumavel.co/lumavelshoppingplaza)**

- Individual shops at `lumavel.co/lumavelshoppingplaza/{your-shop}`
- Listings, variants, inventory, reviews, Stripe checkout
- Shop blog, email campaigns, discount codes, QR codes with optional analytics
- Seller dashboard: analytics, social posting hooks, SEO helpers

**Why sellers switch:** lower platform take on sales, transparent fees, and tools that treat your shop as the product — not the platform’s ad inventory.

## Contribution philosophy

1. **Public repos** — Issues and PRs welcome when the README says so. Keep changes scoped; no drive-by refactors.
2. **Private core** — The main Lumavel product codebase is not open for contribution; feature work happens through our team and trusted collaborators.
3. **Safety** — Never commit secrets, API keys, `.env` files, or customer data. Use issues for security reports (no public exploits).
4. **Respect** — Family-first, faith-rooted brand. Be constructive; no harassment or spam in issues or Discord.
5. **Seller-first lens** — If a change does not help independent creators or sellers, it probably does not belong in our public examples.

We publish **small, high-quality** open material — not everything we build.
